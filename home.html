About Genesis Omnes

Genesis Badaguas-Omnes is a Certified Wealth Planner Â® and Certified Estate Planner in the Philippines. She obtained her certifications from the Wealth Management Center for Communications and Research Inc. She is a graduate of Office Management at STI College, Ortigas-Cainta Campus.

She has been in the Life Insurance industry for 15 years, having worked with Cocolife. She is also a member of the Life Underwriters Association of the Philippines (LUAP). At present, she is one of the Agency Sales Leaders with Cocolife managing a group of Financial Advisors in her team.

Genesis has committed majority of her life understanding the financial standing of a Filipino and has brought to light and shared her knowledge how to best manage money matters. She has also conducted some Financial Literacy talks at the STI College Ortigas-Cainta Campus, LPU Batangas, Taytay Church of Christ Young Adults, and others.

Her goal is to be able to empower and educate Filipinos how to handle finances and how to be debt free.
